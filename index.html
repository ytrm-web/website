About the Resort

Nature, heritage, and thoughtful hospitality in one destination.

Surrounded by paddy fields, areca nut trees, and beautifully landscaped pathways, Yamunatheeram feels immersive from the moment you arrive. The property balances traditional Kerala character with comfortable modern stays, an ayurvedic wellness centre, and serene shared spaces.

At Yamunatheeram, every path, pavilion, and cottage is shaped by the surrounding grove, creating a stay that feels private, elegant, and deeply connected to nature.

From the welcoming reception to the heritage pond, restful interiors, and ayurvedic wellness treatments, the resort offers a calm atmosphere for family getaways, romantic escapes, wellness breaks, and meaningful celebrations.

Ayurvedic Wellness Centre

Ancient healing traditions rooted in Kerala's living landscape.

Our on-site Ayurvedic Wellness Centre brings together traditional Kerala healing wisdom with the serene natural surroundings of the resort. Experienced therapists guide each treatment using authentic herbal oils, time-honoured techniques, and personalised care that restores balance to body, mind, and spirit.

Whether you seek deep relaxation, detoxification, pain relief, or a holistic wellness retreat, our centre offers a curated menu of classical Ayurvedic therapies in a calm, nature-immersed setting.

Abhyanga

Traditional Full-Body Oil Massage

A deeply nourishing warm oil massage performed with rhythmic strokes that improve circulation, calm the nervous system, and release stored tension from muscles and joints.

Relaxation Circulation Detox

Shirodhara

Continuous Oil Flow on the Forehead

A signature Ayurvedic therapy where a steady stream of warm herbal oil flows over the forehead, deeply calming the mind and promoting mental clarity, better sleep, and emotional balance.

Stress relief Mental clarity Sleep

Panchakarma

Five-Action Deep Detox Programme

The gold standard of Ayurvedic detoxification — a multi-day programme that systematically cleanses accumulated toxins from the body and restores vitality through five therapeutic actions.

Deep detox Rejuvenation Immunity

Pizhichil

Royal Oil Bath Therapy

Once reserved for Kerala royalty, Pizhichil involves continuous pouring of warm medicated oil across the entire body while being gently massaged — deeply soothing for joints, muscles, and the nervous system.

Joint care Nervous system Luxury

Njavarakizhi

Medicated Rice Bolus Treatment

A unique Kerala therapy using boluses filled with specially cooked medicinal rice and herbal milk. The warm boluses are pressed rhythmically across the body to nourish skin, ease stiffness, and strengthen tissues.

Skin nourish Strength Anti-aging

Elakizhi

Herbal Leaf Bundle Therapy

Fresh medicinal leaves, herbs, and lemon are bundled, heated in medicated oil, and applied in rhythmic strokes. Excellent for inflammation, arthritis, sports recovery, and overall body rejuvenation.

Pain relief Inflammation Recovery

All treatments are administered by experienced Ayurvedic therapists using authentic Kerala herbal formulations.
